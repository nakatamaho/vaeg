## FDD-visible scheduling gate

The separate `scheduling` evidence suite is deliberately excluded from the
green CTest allowlist. With bytes `18 02 00 00 d3 f4`, A=`0x5a`, and shared
clock slices `1,7`, the legacy 7-clock taken JR leaves a balance of `-6`; the
second slice supplies one positive clock and executes `OUT (0xf4),A`. The new
12-clock JR leaves `-11`; after the same slice it remains at `0x0004` with no
I/O event. The first divergent checkpoint is
`cycle-fdd-io-scheduling/fdd-io-slice/0`:

```text
legacy: pc=0006 r=02 events=read 0004:d3, read 0005:f4, out f4:5a
new:    pc=0004 r=01 events=none
```

Port `0xf4` calls `fdcsubsys_o_dskctl` in the production subsystem. The
difference therefore changes FDD-visible `Exec()` scheduling and blocks G38
under the approved clock policy. It is explained, but not accepted or
allowlisted.
